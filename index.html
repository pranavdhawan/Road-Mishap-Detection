Detection of Road Mishaps

Project Description

The Detection of Road Mishaps project aims to develop a comprehensive system for detecting and preventing road accidents. The project utilizes Internet of Things (IoT) technology to create three essential modules: Speed Calculator, Gas Detection, and Crash Detection.

By combining these modules, the project aims to improve road safety by providing real-time data and alerts to both drivers and authorities, facilitating proactive measures to prevent mishaps.

Modules

Speed Calculator

The Speed Calculator module utilizes sensors and distance calculation algorithms to measure and display the distance between vehicles on the road. This information can help drivers maintain a safe distance and avoid potential collisions.

Gas Detector

The Gas Detection module incorporates gas sensors to detect the presence of any possible leakage within the vehicle.

Crash Detection

The Crash Detection module utilizes GPS and motion sensors to calculate the speed of the vehicle. By monitoring the vehicle's speed, it assists drivers in adhering to speed limits and avoiding dangerous situations caused by excessive speed.
